## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability within Kinetic-RT, please report it privately to our security team. **Please do not disclose the vulnerability publicly or create public GitHub issues until coordinated remediation has been completed.**

### What to Include in Your Report

When reporting a vulnerability, please include as much information as possible to help us reproduce and fix the issue. A good report should include:

* A description of the vulnerability and its potential impact.
* The version(s) of Kinetic-RT affected.
* Detailed steps to reproduce the issue (including any code, scripts, or configurations).
* Any potential workarounds or suggested mitigations.

### Expected Response Timeline

* We will acknowledge receipt of your vulnerability report within 3 business days.
* We will provide a preliminary assessment and estimated timeline for a fix within 7 business days.
* We aim to resolve critical vulnerabilities as quickly as possible and will keep you informed of our progress.

Thank you for helping keep Kinetic-RT secure!
